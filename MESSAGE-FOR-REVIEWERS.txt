Subject: Please review — US ↔ India market correlation app

Hi,

I've built a small web app for exploring correlations between US and India
market & macro metrics, and I'd value your review.

Live link:  [PASTE YOUR https://...onrender.com URL HERE]

You don't need to install anything — just open the link in a browser.

Note: if it's the first time anyone's opened it in a while, the page may take
30–60 seconds to load the first time (the free host puts it to sleep when idle).
After that it's quick.

What to try:
  • Toggle metrics on/off using the chips at the top.
  • Switch between "Levels" and "YoY moves" (top right) — YoY moves is the more
    honest measure of whether two things actually move together.
  • "Correlation matrix" tab: see all relationships at a glance; the panel on the
    right ranks the strongest US↔India links. Click any to inspect it.
  • "Pair & lead-lag" tab: pick any two metrics; the lead/lag chart tests whether
    one tends to move BEFORE the other.
  • "Rolling correlation" tab: shows how a relationship changes over time
    (useful for spotting when two markets decoupled).
  • "Normalized overlay" tab: rebases everything to 100 to compare growth paths.

Data is pulled live from FRED (US markets, rates, commodities, USD/INR) and the
World Bank (India & US macro).

Feedback I'm looking for:
  • Are the right metrics included? Anything missing you'd want?
  • Do the analyses surface anything useful / are they clear?
  • Anything confusing in the UI?

A caveat I'd flag myself: the data is annual, so samples are small — the app is
built for generating hypotheses to investigate, not trading signals. It's not
investment advice.

Thanks!
